# 🏙️ NYC Airbnb Investment Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the New York City Airbnb market to identify key factors influencing listing prices and demand.

The goal is to derive actionable insights that can support investment decisions in the short-term rental market.

---

## Objective

➡️ Where are the best investment opportunities in the NYC Airbnb market?

---

## Key Questions
- Which boroughs have the highest prices?
- How does room type affect pricing?
- Is there a relationship between price and demand (reviews)?
- Which neighborhoods offer the best price-demand balance?
- What factors indicate strong investment potential?

---

## 📊 Dataset
- Source: NYC Airbnb Open Data (Kaggle)
- Contains information on listings, prices, location, availability, and reviews

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Matplotlib & Seaborn (EDA)
- Folium (interactive maps)
- Tableau (dashboard visualization)

---

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

The analysis includes:
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Handling missing values
- Price distribution analysis
- Borough and neighborhood comparisons
- Demand analysis using reviews
- Identification of key drivers for pricing

---

## Interactive Neighborhood Map

This interactive map visualizes Airbnb performance across NYC neighborhoods.

Each marker provides:
- Number of listings
- Average and median price
- Demand indicators (reviews)
- Most common room type
- Availability and stay requirements

👉 The map helps identify high-performing areas and investment opportunities.

---

## Key Insights

- Manhattan has the highest prices but not always the strongest demand
- Brooklyn offers a strong balance between price and demand
- Entire homes generate higher prices but may have lower booking frequency
- High prices do not necessarily correlate with higher demand
- Some neighborhoods outperform others despite lower average prices

---

## Business Recommendation

➡️ Brooklyn presents a more attractive investment opportunity compared to Manhattan due to a better balance of price and demand.

Investors should focus on neighborhoods with:
- Moderate pricing
- High review activity (demand proxy)
- Reasonable availability and stay requirements
